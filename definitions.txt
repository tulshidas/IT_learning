1.IOT
    The Internet of things (IoT) is the extension of Internet connectivity into physical devices 
    and everyday objects. Embedded with electronics, Internet connectivity, and other forms of 
    hardware (such as sensors), these devices can communicate and interact with others over the 
    Internet, and they can be remotely monitored and controlled.
2.Abstration
    the process of removing physical, spatial, or temporal details[2] or attributes in the study of objects or systems in order to more

3.Agile software development
    is an approach to software development under which requirements and solutions evolve through the collaborative effort of 
    self-organizing and cross-functional teams and their customer(s)/end user(s). It advocates adaptive planning, evolutionary 
    development, early delivery, and continual improvement, and it encourages rapid and flexible response to change.
4.JSX
    JavaScript eXtension, or more commonly JSX, is a React extension that allows us to write JavaScript that looks like HTML.
